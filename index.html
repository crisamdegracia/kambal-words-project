About us

We are an independently owned a workshop that specializes in servicing, repairing and restoring a wide variety of glass, aluminum, steel and iron models. We ensure our work meets the highest industry standards. Our goal is to be an provider service leader. We’ll go the extra mile to earn the trust of our clients, and believe in providing the highest level of service in all areas of our expertise. Our craft is our pride, so leave your doors and windows with pros who really care.
